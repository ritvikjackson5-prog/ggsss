About SDG 13

SDG 13 calls for urgent measures to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, build resilience, and integrate climate change measures into national policies.

Key Targets

  • Strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate hazards
  • Integrate climate measures into policies and planning
  • Improve education and awareness on climate action
