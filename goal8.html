About SDG 8

SDG 8 encourages policies for sustainable economic growth, decent job creation, entrepreneurship, and productivity improvements.

Key Targets

  • Promote sustainable economic growth and employment
  • Protect labor rights and promote safe working environments
  • Support youth employment and entrepreneurship
