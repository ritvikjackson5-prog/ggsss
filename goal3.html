About SDG 3

SDG 3 focuses on reducing mortality rates, combating diseases, and promoting mental and physical well-being globally.

Key Targets

  • Reduce maternal and child mortality
  • End epidemics of major communicable diseases
  • Ensure access to essential medicines and vaccines
