About SDG 1

SDG 1 aims to eradicate extreme poverty for all people everywhere by 2030. Key targets include reducing the number of people living in poverty and ensuring access to basic resources.

Key Targets

  • End extreme poverty (people living on less than $1.25/day)
  • Implement social protection systems for all
  • Equal rights to economic resources and services
