About SDG 11

SDG 11 promotes safe, inclusive, and sustainable urbanization and human settlements, with focus on housing, transport, and disaster resilience.

Key Targets

  • Ensure access to adequate, safe and affordable housing
  • Develop sustainable transport systems
  • Reduce adverse environmental impact of cities
