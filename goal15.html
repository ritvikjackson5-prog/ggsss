About SDG 15

SDG 15 focuses on protecting biodiversity, forests, combating desertification, and halting ecosystem degradation.

Key Targets

  • Conserve and restore forests and terrestrial ecosystems
  • Halt biodiversity loss and extinction
  • Combat desertification and land degradation
