About SDG 5

SDG 5 focuses on ending discrimination, violence, and harmful practices against women and girls, ensuring equal participation in society.

Key Targets

  • Eliminate all forms of discrimination and violence
  • Ensure equal access to education, healthcare, and employment
  • Promote leadership and political participation for women
