Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all

About SDG 4

SDG 4 aims to ensure that all children and adults have access to quality education, literacy, and lifelong learning opportunities.

Key Targets

  • Free primary and secondary education for all
  • Increase literacy rates among youth and adults
  • Equal access to vocational and higher education
