About SDG 10

SDG 10 aims to reduce income and social inequalities by empowering marginalized groups and ensuring equal opportunities for all.

Key Targets

  • Reduce income inequalities within and among countries
  • Empower and promote social, economic and political inclusion
  • Ensure equal opportunity and reduce discrimination
