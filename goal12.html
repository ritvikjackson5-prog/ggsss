About SDG 12

SDG 12 encourages sustainable management and efficient use of natural resources and reduction of waste generation.

Key Targets

  • Reduce waste generation through prevention, reduction, recycling and reuse
  • Promote sustainable practices in businesses and consumers
  • Support sustainable public procurement policies
