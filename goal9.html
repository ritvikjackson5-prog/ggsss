About SDG 9

SDG 9 promotes innovation, industrial development, and resilient infrastructure to foster economic growth and sustainable development.

Key Targets

  • Upgrade infrastructure and retrofit industries
  • Support sustainable industrialization and small enterprises
  • Increase research and technological capabilities
