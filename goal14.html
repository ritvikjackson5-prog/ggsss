About SDG 14

SDG 14 focuses on reducing marine pollution, protecting marine ecosystems, and ensuring sustainable fisheries.

Key Targets

  • Prevent and reduce marine pollution
  • Protect marine ecosystems and biodiversity
  • Regulate sustainable fishing practices
