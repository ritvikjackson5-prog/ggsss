About SDG 6

SDG 6 focuses on providing safe drinking water, improving sanitation, and promoting sustainable water management worldwide.

Key Targets

  • Universal access to safe drinking water and sanitation
  • Improve water quality and reduce pollution
  • Increase water-use efficiency across all sectors
