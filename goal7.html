About SDG 7

SDG 7 promotes universal access to modern energy services, increasing renewable energy share and energy efficiency.

Key Targets

  • Universal access to electricity and clean cooking
  • Increase the share of renewable energy in global energy mix
  • Improve energy efficiency across all sectors
