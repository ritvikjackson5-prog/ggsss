About SDG 2

SDG 2 seeks to end hunger and ensure that all people, especially children, have access to sufficient and nutritious food all year round.

Key Targets

  • End all forms of malnutrition
  • Ensure access to safe, nutritious, sufficient food
  • Support sustainable agriculture and rural development
