About SDG 17

SDG 17 emphasizes global partnerships, international cooperation, finance, trade, technology, and capacity building to achieve all other SDGs.

Key Targets

  • Mobilize financial resources for developing countries
  • Enhance technology sharing and capacity building
  • Promote multi-stakeholder partnerships
