About SDG 16

SDG 16 focuses on reducing violence, promoting justice, and building effective, accountable, and inclusive institutions.

Key Targets

  • Reduce all forms of violence
  • Ensure equal access to justice for all
  • Build accountable and inclusive institutions at all levels
